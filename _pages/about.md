I am currently an associate professor at the State Key Laboratory of Virtual Reality Technology and Systems, School of Computer Science and Engineering, Beihang University. I obtained my Ph.D degree from Beihang University in November 2021, supervised by Prof. <a href='http://cvteam.net/members/lijia/upload/index.html'>Jia Li</a>. From November 2021 to January 2024, he engaged in Boya postdoctoral research at the National Engineering Research Center of Visual Technology at Peking University, working with Prof. <a href='https://www.pkuml.org/staff/yhtian.html'>Yonghong Tian</a>. 

My research interest includes multi-modal visual content understanding and AR/VR generation, fine-grained analysis and recognition of objects. I have published more than 27 CCF-A papers such as TPAMI, IJCV, CVPR/ICCV, and NeurIPS and was awarded the Outstanding Reviewer of ICCV2021 and NeurIPS2023, and the Champion of FGVC8-iMET Challenge of the CVPR2021 Conference. <a href='https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=bUzykm0AAAAJ'>https://img.shields.io/badge/Google-Scholar-blue
 Google Scholar</a>
